How to read this

Every feature below was surfaced by at least one teacher during a focus-group session. Bars show the number of distinct teachers who raised each feature, on a scale from 0 to 20. Click any row to see the teachers who raised it, the sessions it surfaced in, and a representative quote.

A teacher is counted once per feature even if they returned to the point later in the same session. Spoken statements and explicit chat agreement (“same as Amy,” “I agree,” “flexible”) count as a mention; a thumbs-up emoji or silent nod does not. Counts are conservative: when a teacher’s position was ambiguous, they were not counted.

Source: facilitator-led focus-group transcripts run by Khan Academy Kids and MDRC, April 7–15, 2026. 6 sessions · ~38 distinct teachers · 5 facilitators (Holly Lavin, Marysela Garza Granados, Brenna Healy, Mervett Hefyan, Marta Benito-Gomez).
